Greece Arcadia SpikeBall

SpikeBall is a made up game created in Mrs. Macbride room primary played around the back table.
The goal of the game is to get the ball to the other side while bouncing once on the table.
The rules are basically gentlemans rules.
But some common rules are, ball cant touch celling, and ball must cross half way for the point to count.
